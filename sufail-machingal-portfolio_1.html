Luxury Jewellery Brand · Brand Identity & E-commerce Design

The Problem

A premium brand with a generic presence

Amana Gold & Diamond was selling high-value jewellery but their branding felt indistinguishable from dozens of local competitors. The client needed an identity that justified premium pricing and built instant trust with affluent buyers — both in-store and online.

Client Goal
Reposition as a luxury brand. Attract high-value customers and justify premium pricing through design credibility.
My Role
Brand strategy, logo design, full identity system, packaging concept, and e-commerce UI/UX design.

Research & Direction

Finding the visual language of trust

Analysed luxury jewellery brands across UAE, India, and Europe. The insight: the most trusted brands use geometric precision, deep dark backgrounds, and restrained gold — never loud or flashy. Heritage cues (subtle arabesque geometry) combined with modern minimalism became the creative direction.

Community Platform · UX

Wellness & Sustainability Community · Platform Design & Brand Identity

The Challenge

Building a real community from scratch

BIO360 is a community platform centred on sustainable living, wellness, and bio-conscious choices. The challenge was designing a digital space that felt warm, human, and trustworthy — not corporate — while handling complex features like events, forums, and member profiles.

Platform Goals
Create belonging. Make it easy to discover events, connect with members, and share sustainable living tips.
Design Challenge
Balance richness of features with simplicity. The audience ranges from students to senior wellness practitioners.

Brand Identity

Organic geometry meets digital clarity

The BIO360 mark is a network of nodes forming a circle — representing connection, community, and the 360° holistic approach. Deep forest greens anchor the palette, referencing nature without feeling rustic. Clean sans-serif type keeps it modern and accessible.
